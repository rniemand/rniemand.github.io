This blog is a dumping ground for my thoughts, applications, technical wins, research and a whole lot more.

I am a self taught developer who is passionate about programming, iot, software engineering and tech in general. I have worked my way up from a computer technician (in 2005) to my first software development job in about 2006 where I worked as a PHP developer.

In December of 2008 I was hired by my current company [Derivco](https://derivco.com/) where I have been provided with the tools and training to become a **Senior Software Developer** working with some of the coolest technologies out there.

I have done a lot of travelling with Derivco and have been in the fortunate position to go overseas multiple times for training and conferences where I have worked with technologies such as [ElasticSearch](https://www.elastic.co/), [ArcSight](https://www.microfocus.com/en-us/cyberres/secops), [.Net](https://dotnet.microsoft.com/en-us/) and more.

At the end of 2017 my family immigrated to Canada to be closer to my past wife's family as she was missing them. I was offered the option to contract back to Derivco which I immediately jumped on and have been working for them ever since.

I run a number of projects on [Github](https://github.com/rniemand) which I would love contributions towards and am always open for comments and pull requests.

At home I manage my own NAS running [Unraid](https://unraid.net/) where I host the majority of my applications, [Docker containers](https://hub.docker.com/u/niemandr), source code, media files and my [Home Assistant](https://www.home-assistant.io/) server. I love home automation and have pretty much got my entire home automated as a result, this has led me into working with [Arduino](https://www.arduino.cc/) and [ESPHome](https://esphome.io/) which I love.

I am always up for a challenge and welcome any feedback, feel free to have a look around and let me know what you think!
